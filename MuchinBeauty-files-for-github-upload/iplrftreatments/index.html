Frequently Asked

IPL & RF Treatments questions.

What can IPL and RF treat?+

IPL targets pigmentation, sun damage, redness and broken capillaries. RF tightens skin and stimulates collagen. Combined in TRIBELLA, they deliver complete facial rejuvenation.

How many sessions do I need?+

Most concerns improve over a series of 3–6 sessions, with results building progressively. We'll personalize your plan at consultation.

Is there downtime?+

Most clients experience only mild redness that settles within a day. RF microneedling may have slightly longer recovery, which we'll review with you.

Is it safe?+

Yes. The Venus Versa platform is Health Canada–licensed and our specialists tailor every setting to your skin for safe, effective treatment.
