Frequently Asked

Japanese Head Spa questions.

What is a Japanese head spa?+

It's a wellness ritual that combines deep scalp cleansing, nourishing treatments and rhythmic massage. It supports scalp and hair health while delivering deep relaxation and stress relief.

What are the benefits?+

A Japanese head spa detoxifies the scalp, can support healthier hair, relieves tension and stress, improves circulation and provides a deeply calming, restorative experience.

How long does it take?+

Choose from 30, 45, 60 or 90-minute experiences depending on how much time and relaxation you want.

How often should I come?+

Many clients enjoy a head spa monthly as part of their self-care and scalp-health routine, though it's wonderful any time you need to reset.
