Frequently Asked

Facial questions.

Which facial is right for me?+

That's exactly what your skin analysis determines. Tell us your goals — clearing acne, anti-aging, glow for an event — and we'll recommend the perfect treatment.

How often should I get a facial?+

For healthy, glowing skin we recommend a facial every 4 weeks, though we'll tailor the frequency to your skin and treatment type.

Is there downtime?+

Most facials have no downtime. Resurfacing treatments like microneedling may cause temporary redness, which we'll review with you beforehand.

What products do you use?+

We use professional-grade skincare including our partner brand Oxygen Botanicals, chosen for clinical-quality, oxygen-enriched formulations.
