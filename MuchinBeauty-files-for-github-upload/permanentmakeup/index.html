Frequently Asked

Permanent Makeup questions.

How long does permanent makeup last?+

Results typically last 1–3 years depending on the treatment, your skin and your lifestyle. A periodic touch-up keeps colour fresh.

Does it hurt?+

We apply a topical numbing agent to keep you comfortable. Most clients find the experience very tolerable.

Will my brows look natural?+

Yes. Microblading creates fine, hair-like strokes that blend with your natural brows. We map the shape with you before we begin.

How should I care for the area while it heals?+

We provide detailed aftercare instructions. Keeping the area clean and avoiding excess moisture and sun during healing protects your results.
