Frequently Asked

Vitamin Therapy questions.

What's in the IV drips?+

Our drips contain combinations of vitamins, minerals, antioxidants and electrolytes tailored to goals like hydration, energy, immunity and skin health. We'll review the formula with you at your consultation.

Is IV vitamin therapy safe?+

Yes — treatments are administered by qualified staff in a clinical setting. We review your health history first to ensure the right formula for you.

How often can I get a drip?+

Frequency depends on your goals and formula. Many clients enjoy regular sessions for ongoing energy, immunity and wellness support.

How long does a session take?+

Booster shots take just minutes. IV drips typically take 30–60 minutes while you relax comfortably.
