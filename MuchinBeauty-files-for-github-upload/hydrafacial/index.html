The Hydrafacial™ at Muchin Beauty Clinic uses patented vortex technology to deliver a deep cleanse, gentle extraction and intense hydration in a single relaxing session. Unlike a traditional facial, the Hydrafacial simultaneously removes impurities and infuses your skin with nourishing serums — so you walk out glowing.

It's suitable for every skin type and addresses fine lines, congestion, dullness, oiliness and uneven texture. With no downtime, it's the perfect treatment before an event or as part of your monthly routine.

Frequently Asked

Hydrafacial™ questions.

Is there any downtime after a Hydrafacial?+

None. The Hydrafacial is gentle and non-invasive — you can apply makeup and return to your day immediately, glowing.

How often should I get a Hydrafacial?+

For best results we recommend one treatment every 4 weeks to maintain healthy, hydrated skin year-round.

Is it suitable for sensitive skin?+

Yes. The Hydrafacial is suitable for all skin types and tones, including sensitive skin. We customize the serums to your needs.

What's the difference between the three tiers?+

All include cleanse, extract and hydrate. Deluxe adds a targeted booster; Platinum adds lymphatic drainage and the full booster experience.
