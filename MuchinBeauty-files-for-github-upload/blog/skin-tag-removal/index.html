Skin Tag Removal: Safe, Fast & Scar-Minimal

Muchin Beauty Clinic · Richmond Hill

Skin tags are harmless, but they can catch on clothing and jewellery and many people simply don't like the look of them. The good news: removing them is quick, precise and minimally invasive.

How removal works

At Muchin we use a JetPen high-frequency device. It delivers a tiny, controlled current to the base of the skin tag, removing it cleanly while minimising impact on the surrounding skin. The precision of the device is what helps keep scarring minimal.

What to expect at the appointment

After a quick assessment to confirm the spots are suitable for treatment, the area is cleansed and each tag is treated in turn. Most appointments are short, and clients typically describe only a brief, mild sensation. Several tags can often be addressed in one visit.

Healing & aftercare

  • A small scab may form over each treated spot — let it heal naturally and don't pick it.
  • Keep the area clean and dry, and apply any aftercare we recommend.
  • Protect the healing skin from sun with SPF.

JetPen removal is Health Canada approved and performed by trained hands at Muchin. If you're unsure whether a spot is a skin tag, we'll always assess it first and refer you on if anything needs a doctor's review.
