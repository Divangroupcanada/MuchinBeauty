Laser hair removal at Muchin Beauty Clinic in Richmond Hill delivers permanent hair reduction by targeting the pigment in the hair follicle with precise pulses of light. Over a series of sessions, treated follicles are disabled — leaving you with smooth, low-maintenance skin and freedom from daily shaving and painful waxing.

What to Expect

1

Consultation & Patch Test

We assess your skin and hair, then perform a patch test to confirm settings.

2

Treatment Session

Pulses of laser energy disable hair follicles. Sessions range from minutes to under an hour by area.

3

Series

Hair grows in cycles, so a series of sessions every 6–8 weeks delivers the best results.

4

Maintenance

Most clients need occasional touch-ups to maintain permanently reduced hair.

Frequently Asked

Laser Hair Removal questions.

How many sessions will I need?+

Most areas need a series of sessions spaced 6–8 weeks apart, because hair grows in cycles and the laser is most effective on actively growing hair. We'll map out your plan at consultation.

Does laser hair removal hurt?+

Most clients describe a mild snapping sensation. Modern systems include cooling to keep treatment comfortable.

Does it work on all skin tones?+

We tailor the laser settings to your individual skin tone and hair type to treat a wide range of skin tones safely and effectively.

Is the hair reduction permanent?+

Laser hair removal delivers permanent hair reduction. Most clients enjoy long-lasting smoothness with occasional maintenance sessions.
