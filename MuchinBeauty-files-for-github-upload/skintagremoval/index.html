Frequently Asked

Skin Tag Removal questions.

Is skin tag removal painful?+

Most clients feel only minor discomfort. The JetPen treatment is quick and precise, and we keep you comfortable throughout.

Will it leave a scar?+

The JetPen is designed for minimal scarring. Following our aftercare guidance helps the area heal cleanly.

Why not remove skin tags at home?+

At-home methods carry risks of infection, scarring and incomplete removal. Professional treatment with a Health Canada–approved device is safer and more reliable.

How many sessions will I need?+

Most skin tags are removed in a single session. Larger or multiple tags may need a follow-up, which we'll discuss at your consultation.
