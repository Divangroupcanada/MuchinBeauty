Frequently Asked

Cosmetic Injection questions.

Does getting Botox or fillers hurt?+

Most clients describe only a mild pinch. We use very fine needles, and numbing cream is available for filler treatments to keep you comfortable.

How long do results last?+

Botox typically lasts 3–4 months. Dermal fillers last 6–18 months depending on the product and treatment area. We'll recommend a maintenance schedule during your consultation.

Will I look frozen or overdone?+

No. Our philosophy is natural enhancement. We tailor dosing to your anatomy so you look refreshed and expressive — never artificial.

When will I see results?+

Filler results are visible immediately. Botox begins working in 3–7 days, with full effect around two weeks.

Is there any downtime?+

Cosmetic injections require essentially no downtime. You can return to most normal activities right away, avoiding strenuous exercise for 24 hours.
