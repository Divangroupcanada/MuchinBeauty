Frequently Asked

Massage Therapy (RMT) questions.

Do you offer direct billing?+

Yes. Because our therapists are Registered Massage Therapists, we offer direct billing to many extended-health insurance providers for your convenience.

What types of massage do you offer?+

Swedish relaxation, deep tissue and myofascial release, sports massage and lymphatic drainage — your RMT will recommend the right approach for you.

How long are sessions?+

Sessions are typically available in 30, 45, 60 or 90-minute lengths. We'll help you choose based on your needs.

Is massage covered by insurance?+

RMT treatment is covered by many extended-health plans. We recommend checking your coverage; we can bill many providers directly.
