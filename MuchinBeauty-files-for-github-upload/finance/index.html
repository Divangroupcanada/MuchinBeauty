Financing & Payment Options.

Flexible, transparent ways to invest in your skin — including direct insurance billing for RMT.

Book Free Consultation

Great skin is an investment — and we want it to fit your life. Muchin offers straightforward, flexible ways to pay, so you can focus on results instead of logistics.

Pay Your Way

We accept all major credit and debit cards, plus contactless payment. Treatment packages can be paid per-session or upfront.

Financing Available

For larger treatment plans, financing options may be available to spread the cost into manageable monthly payments. Ask us during your consultation.

Insurance & Direct Billing

Registered Massage Therapy (RMT) is eligible for insurance — we offer direct billing to most major providers, so you may pay little to nothing out of pocket.

How it works

Every plan starts with a complimentary consultation. We assess your skin and goals, recommend the right treatments, and walk you through pricing clearly — no pressure and no surprises. From there we'll map the most cost-effective path, whether that's a single treatment, a package, or a longer plan paid over time.

RMT direct insurance billing

If you have extended health benefits that cover Registered Massage Therapy, we can bill your insurer directly at the time of your appointment. Bring your insurance details to your first visit and we'll take care of the paperwork. Coverage varies by plan, so we'll always confirm what's eligible before you book.

Questions about cost?

We're happy to talk through pricing and payment before you commit to anything. Call us at (905) 780-0003 or book a free consultation and we'll build a plan that works for your budget.
