Wood therapy (maderoterapia) at Muchin Beauty Clinic in Richmond Hill is a hands-on, completely natural body-contouring treatment. Using specially shaped wooden tools, our therapists apply rhythmic massage techniques to stimulate circulation, support lymphatic drainage and help smooth the appearance of cellulite — no machines, no needles, no downtime.

It is a relaxing, restorative session that sculpts and tones while you unwind. Each plan is tailored to your body and goals during a complimentary consultation.

Frequently Asked

Wood Therapy questions.

What is wood therapy?+

Wood therapy (maderoterapia) is a natural body-contouring massage that uses specially shaped wooden tools to stimulate circulation, support lymphatic drainage and smooth the look of cellulite — with no machines or needles.

Is it painful?+

No. It is a firm but relaxing massage. Most clients find it soothing, and there is no downtime afterward.

How many sessions will I need?+

Results build over a series of sessions. We will recommend a personalized plan during your complimentary consultation.

Is there any downtime?+

None. Wood therapy is completely non-invasive — you can return to your normal activities immediately.
