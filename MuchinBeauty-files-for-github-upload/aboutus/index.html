About Muchin.

A boutique medical aesthetic clinic in Richmond Hill, where 20+ years of expertise meets quiet luxury — and every face is treated like the only one.

Our Story

Muchin Beauty Clinic was founded on a simple belief: that advanced aesthetic care should be backed by science, delivered with genuine warmth, and tailored to the individual. For more than two decades, our team has helped clients across the Greater Toronto Area look and feel like the best version of themselves.

From our Richmond Hill clinic, we offer eleven medical-grade treatments — from cosmetic injections and Hydrafacial to laser hair removal, IPL/RF rejuvenation, permanent makeup, body contouring and the GTA's authentic Japanese head spa. Every treatment begins with listening, and a complimentary consultation.

Our Philosophy

We don't believe in templated treatments or one-size-fits-all beauty. We believe in natural enhancement — results that look like you, refreshed. Our specialists use only proven, Health Canada–approved technology and professional-grade products, including our partner brand Oxygen Botanicals.

The Muchin Promise

Private. Personalized. Unrushed. When you visit Muchin, you're not a number — you're a guest in a space designed to feel like a sanctuary. That's the difference 20+ years of expertise makes.
